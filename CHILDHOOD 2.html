In this third stage, the human being begins to interact in a social way. It begins with kindergarten, passing through the school period (primary education). At this stage, the child begins to reason, develops cognitive abilities and new skills that will help you to become a citizen of the community where you live.The stage culminates at the age of twelve, giving rise to the fourth stage, adolescence.
