This stage occurs during pregnancy in the womb and begins when the zygote forms (the spermatozoon fertilizes the Ovum ). The zygote is a cell that then increases its size (begins to subdivide into others) until forming the embryo (second week). The complete development of the embryo takes ten weeks. Once this phase is completed, the development of the fetus begins (the human form is defined), which will take place the next seven months in the womb until delivery.
